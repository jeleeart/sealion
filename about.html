About

What Materials Do I Use?

Ruby Red Face & Body Paint

A high quality water-based face paint with a child toy safety rating

Hypo-allergenic and washes off easily with water

Safe for sensitive skin, paraben-free, vegan, and not tested on animals

Bio Glitter

I use cosmetic grade biodegradable glitter made from plant cellulose. We don't want more microplastics in our waterways! Not only is it better for the environment, but it's better for your skin too. Bio glitter is less abrasive than petroleum and metal based glitter, so it is more suitable for sensitive skin.
